Frequently Asked Questions

To register as a student, click on the Register button, fill in your details, select "Student" as your role, and choose your teacher from the dropdown list. Once registered, you'll be able to access the student dashboard.

Teachers can view student performance on their dashboard, which shows test scores, completion rates, and chapter-wise analytics. Detailed performance metrics for each student can be accessed from the Students section.

Students can create custom tests by clicking on "Create Test" in their dashboard, then selecting parameters such as chapter, difficulty level, question types, number of questions, and test duration. The system will generate a random test based on these criteria.

When the test timer runs out, the test is automatically submitted with the answers you've provided so far. Unanswered questions will be marked as incorrect. To avoid this, make sure to submit your test before the timer ends.

On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email address. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. Follow the link in the email to create a new password.
